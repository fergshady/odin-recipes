Juiciest Hamburgers

The Hamburger is one of the most common foods that you'll find across the United States.Known for it's quick prep time and juicy flavor it's a highly approved meal when you add fries or chips. So everly delicious.

ingredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg, jumped (beaten)
  • 3/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 3 tablespoons evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic,minced

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients in order to start making this mouth watering treat.
  2. Preheat a grill for high heat.
  3. Mix the ground beef,egg,bread crumbs,evaporated milk,Worcestershire sauce,cayenne pepper, and garlic in a large bowl using your hands.
  4. Don't try to pronounce Worcestershire sauce.
  5. Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties.
  6. Lightly oil the grill grate. Grill patties until browned and no longer pink (or to own liking),for about 5 minutes per side.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy! Yummmmm.
