Ice Cream, we all know it but here we are; about to make one delicious treat. Ice Cream is a very delicious delicacy so long as you're not lactose intolerant! Haha, there are other forms of milk to use to make ice cream if need be, but if you are not lactose intolerant you are in for a treat that requires no extra steps!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) can chocolate syrup
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter
  • 19 ice cream sandwiches
  • 1 (12 oz) container frozen whipped topping,thaw
  • 1 cup salted peanuts
  1. Pour the cocolate syrup into a medium microwave-safe bowl and microwave until hot, about 2 minutes on high, stopping at every 30 seconds. Do not allow boiling! Stir peanut butter into the hot chocolate until smooth. Allow to cool to room temperature.
  2. Line the bottom of a 9x13 inch dish with a layer of ice cream sandwiches. Spread half the whipped topping over the sandwiches. Spoon half the chocolate mixture over that. Top with half the peanuts. Repeat layers. Freeze until firm, at least 1 hour. Cut into squares to serve.
